Case Studies

Real-world implementations transforming pharmaceutical e-commerce

Amazon Pharmacy

Challenge

Entering the highly regulated pharmaceutical market with a consumer-first approach while navigating complex healthcare regulations.

Innovation

Integrated pharmacy services with Amazon's existing e-commerce platform, leveraging Prime membership for discounts and fast delivery.

Results

Rapid market penetration with transparent pricing and seamless user experience, challenging traditional pharmacy models.

Capsule

Challenge

Simplifying the prescription fulfillment process for urban consumers frustrated with traditional pharmacy wait times.

Innovation

Same-day prescription delivery with a mobile-first approach and free delivery, eliminating pharmacy visits.

Results

Expanded to multiple major cities with high customer satisfaction scores and rapid growth in prescription volume.

1mg (Tata Digital)

Challenge

Providing affordable medication access and healthcare information in price-sensitive emerging markets.

Innovation

Comprehensive platform combining medicine information, price comparison, e-pharmacy, and diagnostic services.

Results

Became one of India's largest digital healthcare platforms with millions of users and strong brand recognition.
